My cause: warm, safe, and dry housing.

  • I spent the summer of 2018 working for Appalachia Service Project, a nonprofit with a goal of eradicating substandard housing in central Appalachia.
  • ASP is located in 26 counties in Appalachia. Volunteers - high schoolers and adults - come to ASP centers for a week and do construction on homes in substandard condition. Those of us who are on staff with ASP manage the projects.
  • Typical projects include insulation, ramps, room additions, roofs, drywall, painting, floor repair, and more.
  • ASP aims to make homes warmer, safer, and drier.
  • Warmer, safer, and drier homes is a cause I feel strongly about. I saw how having a warm, safe, and dry home changed the lives of my homeowners.
