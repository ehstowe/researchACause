My cause: warm, safe, and dry housing.

I spent this past summer working for Appalachia Service Project, a nonprofit with a goal of eradicating substandard housing in Appalachia. ASP is located in 26 counties in Appalachia. Volunteers - high schoolers and adults - come to ASP centers for a week and do construction on homes in substandard condition. Those of us who worked for ASP all summer managed the projects. Typical projects include insulation, ramps, room additions, roofs, drywall, painting, floor repair, and more. ASP works to make homes warmer, safer, and drier. This is a cause I feel strongly about because I saw how having a warm, safe, and dry home changed the lives of my homeowners.
